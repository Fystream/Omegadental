Our story

A clinic built on expertise and care.

Omega Dental Clinic was founded on a simple idea: patients in Pulpally deserve the same quality of dental care available in major cities — delivered by specialist doctors, using modern equipment, in a welcoming environment.

Our chief dental surgeon, Dr. Deepak V., brings advanced training from Germany and a certified clinical research background. Together with three specialist colleagues, the team covers everything from routine check-ups to complex implant and surgical work — all under one roof.

  • Germany-trained chief surgeon
  • In-house orthodontist, oral surgeon & implantologist
  • Strict sterilization & hygiene protocols
  • LGBTQ+ friendly, judgement-free environment
