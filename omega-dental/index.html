Best Dentist in Pulpally, Wayanad — Omega Dental Clinic

Omega Dental Clinic is a leading dental clinic located in Pulpally, Wayanad, Kerala. We serve patients across Wayanad including Sultan Bathery, Kalpetta, and Mananthavady. Our dental services include clear aligners treatment, painless root canal treatment, painless tooth extractions, dental bridges and crowns, smile designing, dental implants, orthodontic braces, pediatric dentistry, and oral maxillofacial surgery. Led by Dr. Deepak V., a Germany-trained Chief Dental Surgeon, the team also includes Dr. Renny Philip, Dr. Bijo Jose (MDS Orthodontist), and Dr. Rakhil R. (MDS Oral Surgeon and Implantologist).
